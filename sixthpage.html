Blue Poison Dart Frog

The blue poison dart frog is terrestrial, living on the edges of water sources. Lacking webbed feet, this frog rarely enters deeper waters.

  • Size 1 – 1.5 inches (2.54 – 3.81 centimeters)
  • Weight 0.11 ounces (3 grams)
  • Habitat Tropical rain forest

Red-eyed Tree Frog

Red-eyed tree frogs are most well known for their huge, bright red eyes.

  1. Size Males: 2 – 2.5 inches (5.1 – 6.4 centimeters) Females: 2.5 - 3 inches(6.4 – 7.6 centimeters)
  2. Weight 0.21 – 0.53 ounces (6 to 15 grams)
  3. Habitat Rain forest

Dyeing Poison Dart Frog

The dyeing poison dart frog gets its name from an old legend in which the green feathers of parrots were changed, or “dyed,” into red feathers.

  • Size 1.57 – 1.97 inches (4 – 5 centimeters)
  • Weight 0.11 ounces (3 grams)
  • Habitat Humid forests
